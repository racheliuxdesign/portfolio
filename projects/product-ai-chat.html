Product AI Chat

Real-time messaging system for product teams to collaborate, share feedback, and streamline communication workflows.

Role Lead Product Designer
Duration 4 months
Team Designer, 3 Engineers, PM
Hero screenshot placeholder

Overview

Product teams needed a seamless way to communicate within the context of their work. The AI Chat feature was designed to integrate real-time messaging directly into the product workflow, enabling faster decision-making and reducing context switching between tools.

Problem

Teams relied on external tools for communication, leading to fragmented conversations and lost context. Important decisions made in chat were disconnected from the product data they referenced, making it difficult to trace rationale and maintain alignment across team members.

User journey map — communication pain points

Design Process

Started with competitive analysis of existing collaboration tools, followed by user interviews to understand communication patterns. Designed an AI-powered chat that could reference product data, suggest actions, and maintain contextual threads. Multiple rounds of prototyping and testing refined the interaction model for both quick messages and deeper discussions.

Design exploration — chat interface iterations
Final design — AI Chat interface

Outcome & Impact

The AI Chat feature became one of the most used collaboration tools within the product. It significantly reduced the time teams spent switching between communication and work tools, and improved decision traceability by keeping conversations connected to the relevant product context.
