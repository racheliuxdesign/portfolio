Enterprise Onboarding Wizard

Designed a guided onboarding flow that reduced time-to-first-value for new enterprise customers from days to hours.

Role Senior UX Designer
Duration 3 months
Team Designer, 2 Engineers, PM, CSM
Hero screenshot placeholder

Overview

New enterprise customers faced a steep learning curve when setting up the platform for the first time. The onboarding process required configuring integrations, defining organizational structure, and setting up initial policies — tasks that previously relied heavily on professional services and support documentation.

Problem

Customer onboarding was a major bottleneck. New accounts took an average of 5–7 business days to become fully operational, requiring multiple support calls and hand-holding. The lack of a structured setup flow led to misconfigured environments and early churn risk, especially among mid-market customers without dedicated IT teams.

Before state — unguided setup experience

Design Process

Mapped the complete onboarding journey by interviewing customer success managers and analyzing support ticket patterns. Identified the critical setup milestones and designed a step-by-step wizard that broke the process into manageable stages with progress tracking, inline validation, and contextual help. Tested with customers at various technical levels to ensure the flow was accessible without being patronizing to experienced admins.

Design exploration — wizard flow and step breakdowns
Final design — guided onboarding wizard

Outcome & Impact

The onboarding wizard reduced average setup time from 5–7 days to under 4 hours for most customers. Support tickets related to initial setup dropped significantly, and customer satisfaction scores for the onboarding experience improved substantially. The wizard also became a key selling point in product demos.
