Cyber Risk Quantification

Goals

  • Evaluate the organization's risks
  • Assess cost and likelihood of breach to each business-critical asset
  • Understand the financial meaning of these possible breaches
  • Communicate risk to board and stakeholders
  • Prioritizing mitigation efforts, and save time and money and reduce risk

Personas

  • CISO
  • C-Level management
  • The decision maker of the organization

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide the CISO with an overview of the organization's risk status. Additionally, this view is used to communicate the status to stakeholders and to secure budgets for risk mitigation efforts.

🌫️

Challenge: Do not make the user panic!

To enhance the accuracy and richness of our system calculations, we ask users a significant number of questions. However, this can be overwhelming! We aim to avoid overwhelming users and causing them to abandon the process before they even begin.

💡

The Solution

Breaking the questions into smaller, manageable chunks proved to be the most effective approach. This allows users to focus on a subset of questions at a time, making the process clearer and less overwhelming. Once the initial form is completed, the view changes, enabling users to easily edit the summary list and search for specific questions using the search box.

🌫️

Challenge: Simplify a very complex algorithm

As users input extensive amounts of data and we automatically collect substantial data, the volume can be overwhelming, leading to confusion and difficulty in grasping the key points. Our objective was to present the bottom line in the simplest manner possible.

💡

The Solution

We created an aggregated list of categories and summed the costs for each category. Utilizing a straightforward "vertical calculation" to reach the bottom line is a familiar method, akin to what users have practiced since grade school—so why not apply it here?

🎯

Challenge: Integrate quantification across the system in a holistic way

We need to seamlessly embed quantification throughout the system to assist users in making informed decisions. This approach will prompt users to consider quantification and encourage its use in every action they take.

💡

The Solution

Analyze users' workflows and behavior patterns, and strategically embed quantification values at critical moments across the system.
